How your writing is saved

Everything you write is saved automatically on your device, in the browser you are using. No data is transmitted to external servers — your content is completely private and accessible only to you.

Please note: since your writing is stored on your device, it may be lost if you change your phone or computer, switch browsers, or clear your browser data. We recommend exporting your journal as a PDF regularly using the built-in export feature.
